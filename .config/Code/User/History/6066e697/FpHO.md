`w`     Move forward to the beginning of the next word
`W`     Move forward to the beginning of the next WORD
`e`     Move forward one word to the end of the next word
`E`     Move forward one word to the end of the next WORD
`b`     Move backward to beginning of the previous word
`B`     Move backward to beginning of the previous WORD
`ge`    Move backward to end of the previous word
`gE`    Move backward to end of the previous WORD
